# Welcome To HazParse!

The life of a scientist can be dangerous at times. Handling a great variety of chemicals puts you at risk to harm yourself, your colleagues or even the environment. To make the lab a safer place, the authorities require to hand out information about safe substance handling  - the Operational Safety Instructions (OSI). 
The OSI is mandatory in Germany and in case you work with people from all around the globe, you are at least required to provide this document in German and English language.

## And this is where the problem begins: 

Every institution has an officer for hazardous substance management - the person that cares for proper documentation, lab safety including storage. For most institutions, this is not the persons’ only job (mostly they are scientists or lab technicians besides that). Currently, the safety officer needs to write the OSI by hand or by copy-pasting from the safety datasheet of the hazardous substance. This leads to two main problems:  

1. The instructions are not written due to the time that it takes (besides other administrational tasks). 

2. The instructions are written, but due to the human factor – the quality and structure depend on the author.  

Having no instructions or low quality instructions leads to people handling chemical substances wrong and/or thereby putting themselves and others at risk.  

## This is where HazParse jumps in:

I was an officer for hazardous substance management myself. Writing the OSIs, I realized that great parts of the OSI are the same as in the substance safety data sheet (SDS) - just draped together in different order. As every substance is delivered with an SDS, we could simple take the information from that .pdf-file and puzzle it together, so that it fits the form of a German OSI. Eventual gaps can be filled with information from trustworthy chemical data bases from the internet.


## Benefits from using/developing HazParse: 

The automation of the writing process leads to following major effects:

1. OSIs exist - as the automated process is simple to implement into the delivery process, there is less chance of substances being overseen.
2. The style of the OSIs is coherent due to the usage of same source of information.
3. OSIs are written in a reasonable amount of time. - The automated process saves time in documentation that instead can be used for actual research or production.
